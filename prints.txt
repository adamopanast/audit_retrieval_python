AUTOMATED FETCH FOR OUD AUDIT FILES
-----------------------------------

step2 : fetch OUD audit files from the given instructions on fetchOUD.conf : `python fetchOUD.py`


BE SURE THAT THE CONFIG FILES CONTAIN THE RIGHT
IPs , USR NAMES , PASSWRDS and PATHS!!!

to terminate : ctrl+c
